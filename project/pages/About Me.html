About NickBass Lessons

Playing at Reverb with Nightfire.

Hello, my name is Nick (Bass) Shaw.

Experience - 8 Years

What interested you in playing bass?: When I first got the idea to play an instrument, my options were guitar or bass. Since my sister already played guitar, I guess I decide to be rebellious and master the bass. What really solidified my decision on playing bass was actually a friend of my dad's, who really wailed on the bass.Ever since then I bought myself a bass and have been playing ever since.

Practical experience (Concert/band): Since my first bass I played for the All County Jazz band, Invalids, Rod De George, Nightfire, Blackcrown Initiate.

Why NickBass?: Honestly, it is a really old nickname that just kind of followed me all these years.
