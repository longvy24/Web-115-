Mission Statement

Hello, my name is Nick Shaw, and I lead NickBass Lessons. What is NickBass Lessons, you ask? It is a splendid departure from the teaching norm… it is more than just shredding to the work of others, but learning to slay on your own. I would like to cordially invite you on a journey into your untapped creative subconscious.

Days in the studio mastering music; nights on working on my projects Invalids and Blackcrown Initiates; and years spent teaching bass players at all levels and disciplines… it has all left me asking why? Why do some bassists (even the pros), ROCK and others not? Turns out, the answer is simpler than you think… it’s all about understanding.

Our musical heroes understand the music that moves them — lock, stock and barrel; they manage to channel this great energy to CREATE the music that moves us.

It may have taken me a lifetime, but I tell you this… Nick Shaw understands; I understand how… and I know why… and guess what…? I’m ready to teach you. So, grab your bass and let’s do this!
